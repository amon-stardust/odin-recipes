Crab and Corn Chowder

Description

This was the first soup I ever made and I loved how it turned out. I've always been a big seafood fan, especially crab and I'm sure most people are too! This is a creamy, flavorful and filling soup that is perfect for the winter or summer.

Ingredients

  • Onions
  • Chicken Stock
  • Olive Oil
  • Butter
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Potatoes
  • Corn
  • Corn Cobs (optional)
  • Salt, Pepper, and Old Bay
  • Flour
  • Crab
